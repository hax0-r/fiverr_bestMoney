Best Pet Insurance Services of 2024

Su Guillory Business and Finance Expert

The best pet insurance policies provide protection and peace of mind for pet owners, but policies vary broadly, so choosing the right coverage for your pet can be time-consuming.

To save you time (and money), we’ve looked at the best pet insurance companies out there, narrowing our list to solid competitors that offer policies for most pets in most parts of the country. Here are our expert picks for the best pet insurance policies:

Methodology: How We Reviewed the Pet Insurance Companies

The pet insurance industry has seen significant growth over the past decade, evolving from a niche market into a mainstream solution for pet owners seeking to manage the rising costs of veterinary care.

To gauge where the industry stands today and what pet insurance providers offer the best options, we looked at a broad range of coverage features, including:

  • Pet insurance rates: These can significantly impact your budget, making it essential to compare policies.
  • Special waiting period: Understanding the waiting periods for different conditions ensures you know when your coverage starts.
  • Direct payment to vet: Direct payments to veterinarians simplify the process, sparing you from paying large sums upfront and waiting for reimbursements.
  • 24/7 vet health line: Access to a 24/7 vet health line provides immediate professional advice, crucial for handling unexpected health issues.
  • Routine wellness plans: Covers preventive care such as vaccinations and check-ups
  • Pays veterinary exam fees: Coverage here can save you significant money, especially if your pet requires frequent vet visits.
  • Euthanasia or end-of-life expenses: Including these expenses in your policy helps manage the financial burden during emotionally challenging times.
  • Multi-pet discount: Lowers the overall cost of insuring multiple pets, making comprehensive coverage more accessible and affordable.

Spot Pet Insurance - Best For Accident and Illness

  • Maximum annual coverage: $2,500 to unlimited
  • Reimbursement choices (%): 70, 80, 90
  • Deductible choices: $100, $250, $500, $750, $1,000
  • BBB rating: A

Whether your pet is well, sick, or hurt, Spot offers some of the most comprehensive pet insurance around. The waiting period for accidents, illnesses, or even ligament and knee conditions is just 14 days, and alternative treatments are covered.

Why We Chose Spot: Few insurers offer the number of covered services that Spot does, including surgery and special care, infectious diseases and diseases from parasites, virtual vet visits, and microchip implantation.

Customer Experience: Customers are thrilled with Spot Pet Insurance, saying claims get paid quickly and customer service reps are helpful and friendly. Negative complaints address the no direct-to-vet payment option and say the mobile app could be better.
